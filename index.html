Free Introduction To Square Dancing

Want more laughter in your life? Looking for a new interest, more social connection, or an activity to share with your partner? Interested in keeping your mind sharp? Or just want to have a free, fun evening doing something different? Then come on out to Squares Across The Border's introduction to square dancing. The first two lessons are FREE.


This is not the square dancing you may expect or remember:

  • Dress is casual
  • Music is contemporary
  • We laugh a lot
  • It's great exercise
  • Singles and couples are welcome
  • Lead? follow? Your choice
  • Absolutely no experience (or rhythm) is necessary
  • Don't know your right from your left? You'll learn!
